What We Tested & How

For this re-audit we shifted from head-term monitoring to long-tail community-and-comparison query probing — the exact terrain the April 2026 content drop was engineered to win. We selected the six most strategically-shipped guides, derived two high-intent buyer queries per guide, and probed each query live against the accessible AI surfaces using a real Chrome browser context (Playwright, May 5, 2026). Every confirmed citation is backed by a full-page screenshot stored in this report's /screenshots/ directory.

Twelve strategic queries were probed across five AI surfaces (Google AI Overviews, Bing Copilot, Perplexity, ChatGPT, Anthropic Claude). Perplexity and Claude were behind login walls during capture, so their synthesized answers could not be confirmed or denied this cycle and are marked Gated.

What's Working — The Four Mechanics Producing Citations

1. The Schema Rebuild Is Compounding

Article + FAQPage + BreadcrumbList + RealEstateAgent + GeoCoordinates + Place ship clean on the April templates. Google AIO and Bing Copilot are synthesizing from the new pages within 30-35 days of publish — so every new guide is a citation candidate from day one.

2. Owned Subdomains Capture Community Queries

addisonreserverealestate.com ranks Google Page 1 for “Addison Reserve homes for sale” AND “Addison Reserve country club homes” — a Koolik-controlled position beside Realtor, Zillow, and Sotheby's. bocawestrealestate.com surfaces in the brand SERP. Replicate for RPYCC, St Andrews, Woodfield.

3. Comparison Content Is the Citation Magnet

Both strongest wins — Google AIO on “Addison Reserve fee 2026” and “Addison Reserve vs Mizner” — come from the comparison cluster. AI surfaces preferentially synthesize structured comparison content. Koolik now owns a seven-piece comparison moat.

4. Brand Entity Strength Halos Every Query

Knowledge Panel completeness, the 5.0 / 64 review baseline, social coverage, and a six-sitelink brand SERP create the entity authority AI surfaces use as a trust signal — which is why the AIO cited “The Koolik Group” by name.

Q3 2026 Growth Runway — Next-Wave Targets

The April drop won the long-tail community and comparison terrain — exactly as engineered. The remaining gaps are not on-site SEO problems; they are directory-locked head queries that on-site content alone cannot dislodge. Here is the candid read and the runway.

The honest read on head queries. “Best real estate team in Boca Raton,” “top realtors in Boca Raton,” and “best place to live in Boca Raton” are answered from FastExpert, U.S. News, Newsweek, Niche, Livability, and Yelp — directory-pattern answer engines. They hold because of review volume (competitor Mastropieri sits at 2,000+ reviews vs Koolik ~67) and third-party editorial placements. This is a moat problem, not a content problem — and the next phases attack it directly.

1. Review Acceleration Program

Post-close NPS + review-request automation. Goal: from ~67 reviews to 250+ in 90 days, 500+ by end of Q3. AI surfaces start surfacing Koolik on “best team” queries once the review-volume signal crosses threshold. Highest-ROI move on the board.

2. Directory Placement Campaign

FastExpert claim + optimization, U.S. News agent ranking, Newsweek / Statista America's Best Realtors, Forbes / Bloomberg market-expert placement. These directories are the source pool ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude read on head queries — get inside them and the head-query citations follow.

3. National Earned Media

Claude and Perplexity weight national editorial heavily. Target WSJ Mansion, Robb Report, Architectural Digest, Business of Home, NYT Real Estate. With $2.7B career volume and a 36-year multigenerational team, Koolik is editorially placeable — the unlock for the two login-walled surfaces.

4. Owned-Subdomain Expansion

addisonreserverealestate.com proved the model. Stand up rpyccrealestate.com, standrewscountryclubrealestate.com, and woodfieldcountryclubrealestate.com on the same architecture — each a Koolik-controlled Page 1 position on the highest-intent community-buy query in that micro-market.

5. Comparison-Cluster Doubling-Down

The highest citation-yield category in the audit. Ship the next wave: “Polo Club vs Mizner,” “Highland Beach vs Boca Raton waterfront,” “St Andrews vs Addison Reserve fees,” and individual club-fee deep-dives. Each is a candidate Google AIO and Bing Copilot citation within 30-60 days of publish.
